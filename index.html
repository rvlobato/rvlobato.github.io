About me

I was born in Igarapé-Miri and raised in Barcarena, two cities of Pará in the north of Brazil, currently in live in São José dos Campos, in the state of São Paulo.

I am a theoretical physicist with interest in nuclear and relativistic astrophysics, my research focuses on the theoretical exploration of the properties of strongly interacting matter at ultra-high densities and temperatures, structure and evolution of compact stars (white dwarfs, neutron stars, pulsars, magnetars, quarks stars, black holes), emission mechanisms (electromagnetic and gravitational) of white dwarfs and neutron stars, ultrastrong electromagnetic fields (production of X and gamma rays) and cosmic rays.​ More information about my academic life you can find in my Researchgate, Curriculum Vitae or Currículo Lattes (in Portuguese).
